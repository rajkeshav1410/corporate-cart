About Corporate Cart

Corporate Cart is a cutting-edge internal marketplace platform designed exclusively for employees of organizations. With a focus on security, efficiency, and user-friendliness, Corporate Cart revolutionizes the way employees buy, sell, and trade personal items within the workplace.

Our platform is tailored to simplify transactions and enhance community bonding within your organization. From user authentication to seamless item management, from transaction handling to advanced search and filtering options, Corporate Cart offers a comprehensive solution to streamline internal trading processes.

Developed using TypeScript and adhering to best practices in Git commits and code quality, Corporate Cart ensures a secure and engaging marketplace experience.

Join us on Corporate Cart and discover a new way to connect, trade, and engage with your colleagues like never before.
